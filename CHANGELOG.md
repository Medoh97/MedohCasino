# Changelog

Newest first. Version numbers match the build shown on the Start/Settings screen.

## Latest build (1.0.151)

**Baccarat**
- **Live running totals** now update as each card is dealt, instead of only showing at the result — easier to follow the coup and the third-card draw decisions.

## 1.0.150

**Changed**
- The **Stats** page is now organised by game and collapsed by default — tap a game to expand its stats, instead of one long scroll.
- **Correct decks per game:** 3 Card Poker, High Card Flush, and Wild Fives are now single-deck (Wild Fives with one joker); Baccarat uses an 8-deck shoe. This also makes High Card Flush's big flush bonuses correctly rare.

## 1.0.149

**New**
- **Sort: Rank | Suit** — in 3 Card Poker, High Card Flush, and Wild Fives, tap to group your cards so pairs, flushes, and straights are easy to spot. It only rearranges the display; your hand is scored the same either way.

## 1.0.148

**Baccarat**
- The result now leads with **PLAYER WINS / BANKER WINS / TIE** — your chip net is shown clearly below — instead of a win/lose label based only on your net (which was misleading when a side bet won on a losing hand).
- A "How it played out" summary, plus a live status during the deal, now explains **why** a third card is drawn.
- New **Step** toggle pauses before each third-card draw so you can follow the drawing rules one step at a time.

## 1.0.146

**New**
- **High Card Flush** — a brand-new game: longest flush wins, with ante / raise / fold and two side bets (Flush Bonus and Straight Flush Bonus).
- Per-game **Stats** and **Achievements** for 3 Card Poker, Baccarat, and High Card Flush.
- **Session log** on the table games — review your recent rounds and your running session net.
- A **Paytable** button on every table game that you can open mid-hand without interrupting play (includes a new Baccarat paytable).
- **Wild Fives** now shows a poker hand-ranking reference while you choose, so you can tell which hand beats which.

**Improved**
- Bigger, more readable cards in High Card Flush and 3 Card Poker.

**Fixed**
- The Blackjack tile in the game grid now launches correctly.
- Corrected the Slots description.

## 1.0.133

**New**
- 96 high-quality phone backgrounds added to the store — new **Luxury** and **Neon** categories, plus a lot more **Nature** and **Space**.
- Real sampled sound effects throughout, a **master volume slider** in Settings, and a **mute button** on every betting screen.
- **Wild Fives auto-play** with a per-session hand log and summary.

**Fixed**
- Blackjack busts could occasionally be counted as a win if you tapped during the deal animation. Player actions are now locked while cards are being dealt.
- Insurance **Yes/No** buttons could end up off-screen on smaller phones — they're now pinned and always reachable, and respond instantly.
- **Martingale / Paroli** auto-bet progressions now actually take effect.
- Split-hand edge cases when tapping quickly during dealing.
- Wild Fives side-bet payouts when the dealer doesn't qualify (Play wins, Bonus pushes, Wild 5 follows its own paytable).
- Wild Fives now uses a single joker.

**Changed**
- Removed the Kingdom Come backgrounds.
- Themed card styles renamed (Adventurer, Old Kingdom, Harvest, Wildcard, Gloom, Synthwave).
- Buster Blackjack shows the dealer's face-up card on the right.
